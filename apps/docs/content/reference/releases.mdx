## 0.6.0-beta.0

- introduces the required explicit `assistantText` terminal-result field
- documents Kestrel One as the hosted product centered on Threads and Projects
- documents organization-scoped model access and managed model deployments
- aligns Desktop, CLI, runtime, protocol, SDK, Next.js, and observability around one Beta line
- organizes guidance around Desktop, Kestrel One, building applications, and operating deployments
