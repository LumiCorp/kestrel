The quality score material gives the repo a compact way to talk about trend and health, not only isolated failures.

## Scoring Dimensions

The current score dimensions include:

- architecture compliance
- test depth
- incident rate
- drift rate
- replay stability
- latency

## Source Of Truth

The scorecard is generated from repo artifacts, and it sits next to drift scans and governance references rather than apart from them.
